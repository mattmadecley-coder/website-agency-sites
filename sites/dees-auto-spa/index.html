What We Do

Our Services

Every service below is available a la carte or bundled together — call and we'll help you build a package that fits your vehicle and your budget.

Auto & Marine Detailing

Hand washing, clay-bar decontamination, and interior deep-cleaning for cars, trucks, and boats — inside and out.

Ceramic Coatings

A long-lasting liquid polymer that bonds to your paint, adding gloss and making the surface far easier to keep clean.

Paint Protection Film

Clear film applied to high-impact areas to shield your paint from rock chips and road debris.

Window Tinting

Heat- and glare-reducing film for your windows, with shade options to match your look and your state's limits.

Bed Liners

Spray-on or drop-in liners that protect your truck bed from scratches, dents, and rust.

Powder Coating

A durable, baked-on finish for wheels and metal parts that resists chipping far better than standard paint.

Paintless Dent Repair

Dents and dings massaged out from behind the panel — no fillers, no repainting, no color-match risk.

Headlight Restoration

Removing the cloudy, yellowed oxidation from old headlights to restore clarity and night-time visibility.

Odor / Water-Spot Removal

Deep-cleaning treatments that lift stubborn smells and hard-water spotting from upholstery, carpet, and glass.

Motorcycle Detailing

The same hand-detailing care, scaled down for bikes — chrome, paint, and leather all addressed.

About Us

Dee's Auto Spa — Ceramic Coatings of Jesup

Established in 2007, Dee's Auto Spa has spent 19 years serving Jesup, GA and Wayne County with full-service auto and marine detailing. From ceramic coatings and paint protection film to window tint, bed liners, and paintless dent repair, the shop covers nearly every job a vehicle owner needs — backed by a 4.7-star reputation across 30 reviews. Whether it's a daily driver, a weekend project, or a boat that's seen a season on the water, the goal is the same: a clean, protected finish and a straightforward, no-surprises experience from quote to pickup.
