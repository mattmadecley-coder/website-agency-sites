What We Do

Our Services

Every service below can be booked on its own or combined into a full protection package — call to talk through what's right for your vehicle.

XPEL Paint Protection Film

A clear, self-healing film applied to your most vulnerable panels to guard against rock chips, road debris, and everyday wear.

Ceramic Coating

A liquid polymer that bonds to your clear coat, adding a deep gloss and making the surface easier to wash and more UV-resistant.

Paint Correction

Multi-stage polishing that removes swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation to restore clarity before protection goes on.

Deep Interior Detailing

A thorough clean of seats, carpets, trim, and glass — lifting stains and odors a regular wash misses.

Window Tint

Heat- and UV-blocking film for your windows, improving comfort and privacy while protecting your interior.

Vehicle Wraps

Full or partial color-change vinyl wraps for a custom look, while protecting the factory paint underneath.

Glass Coating

A hydrophobic coating for windshields that improves visibility in rain and makes grime easier to wipe away.

Wheel & Caliper Coating

Heat-resistant ceramic coatings for wheels and calipers that resist brake dust and stay looking finished.
